Dalin

Dalin brand was founded by pharmacist Adalet Karaağaç, who is also the founder of Bilim Pharmaceuticals, by Kopas Kozmetik Pazarlama ve Sanayi A.Ş. was created in 1983. The brand's goal was to offer products of both high quality and affordable prices, developed and produced with the rigor of medicine, specially for babies with different needs from adults in hair and skin care. For the first time, tear-free baby shampoo, baby powder and baby soap were offered to mothers. Dalin products have been meticulously produced ahead of their time since the first day. dalin production facility is the first production facility to receive the GMP (good manufacturing practice) certificate in the cosmetics industry in Turkey. The quality offered to mothers and as a result of this, the backbone of the trust that has been going on for generations is guaranteed by qas (quality assurance system). Dalin has also expanded its expertise in baby care from hair care to skin care, from products for mothers to baby laundry care; Today, it offers 40 different product types to mothers in 17 different categories such as baby oil, diaper rash cream, fly repellent spray, wet towel, baby care cover, breast pad, baby detergent and softener specially developed for babies and sensitive skin, and meets all the needs of mothers. baby care brand
